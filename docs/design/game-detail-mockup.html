About This Game

Gridiron Tactics: Pro Football Strategy puts you in complete control of an NFL franchise — from the opening coin toss to the fourth-quarter two-minute drill. Every play call, every substitution, every challenge flag is yours to own.

The game uses a card-driven play selection system where your offensive and defensive playbooks are represented by decks of cards, each encoding real-world football concepts: the West Coast offense, the Cover 2 defense, the read-option. As your team builds momentum, your deck grows more powerful. As they tire or get injured, it diminishes.

With a complete 32-team season mode, historical rosters spanning five decades, and a robust draft system, this is the football simulation that serious fans have been waiting for. No dice — just cards, decisions, and a deep understanding of the game.

The head-to-head mode is considered one of the tightest two-player experiences in sports gaming. Each game clocks in at around 90 minutes — exactly as long as a real football game feels when the stakes are this high.
